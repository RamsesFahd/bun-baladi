A Rich Legacy of Arab Culinary Traditions At the heart of the Two Holy Mosques, Bun Makkah reigns supreme as one of the most prominent products of the Arab food heritage. This authentic coffee is inspired by the coffee cultivation and preparation traditions that are an integral part of the cultural identity of Makkah Al-Mukarramah .

How Made


The coffee beans are meticulously sourced using the best sustainable farming practices and skilled handcrafting to preserve their exceptional quality and distinctive characteristics. When roasted with care, Mecca Coffee reveals a spectrum of rich flavors - from the ideal sweetness to the gentle acidity and velvety mouthfeel .
