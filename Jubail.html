About Al Jubail Bun

Al Jubail coffee beans are a testament to the unique terroir of the city, offering a rich and distinct flavor profile that stands out in the world of specialty coffee. Grown in the fertile regions of Al Jubail, these beans benefit from the ideal combination of climate, altitude, and soil composition, which imparts a remarkable depth and complexity to the coffee. What makes Al Jubail coffee beans truly special is their nuanced taste, characterized by subtle hints of berries and a smooth vanilla undertone that lingers on the palate, making each sip an unforgettable experience.

How Made

rafting the perfect cup of Al Jubail coffee begins with meticulous attention to detail at every stage of production. From hand-picking the ripest cherries to employing traditional drying methods, we ensure that each bean retains its full spectrum of flavors. During roasting, we carefully monitor temperature and time to highlight the natural nuances of berries and vanilla inherent in Al Jubail beans. This painstaking process results in a coffee that not only captures the essence of its origin but also delivers a balanced and aromatic brew with every cup.
