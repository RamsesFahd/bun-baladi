About Us

The "Bun Baladi" website is dedicated to promoting and selling 100% locally-produced coffee beans from Saudi Arabia. Our mission is to foster a culture of appreciating and consuming high-quality, homegrown products while supporting the goals of Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 initiative.


Vision 2030 is a comprehensive plan to diversify the Saudi economy and reduce its reliance on oil exports. A key focus of this initiative is to develop the country's agricultural and trade sectors, creating new opportunities for local producers and entrepreneurs.


By choosing Buldi's coffee, you are not only enjoying a premium, freshly roasted product, but you are also actively supporting Saudi farmers and contributing to the growth of the national economy. Our coffee beans are carefully selected from the best growing regions in the Kingdom, ensuring exceptional flavor and aroma.


We take great pride in our role as a homegrown business, and we are committed to providing our customers with a truly authentic Saudi coffee experience. Through our website, we offer a convenient and reliable way for both local and international customers to access our premium coffee products.


In addition to selling coffee, we also aim to educate our customers about the rich history and traditions of coffee production in Saudi Arabia. Our blog features informative articles on the different coffee growing regions, unique brewing methods, and the cultural significance of coffee in the Kingdom.
